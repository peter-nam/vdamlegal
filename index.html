Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 10, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how VDAM ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and handles your data when you use our services and integrate with third-party platforms like Dropbox.

2. Data Collection and Third-Party Integrations (Dropbox)

When you connect VDAM to your Dropbox account, we request specific permissions to access your files. We strictly adhere to the following principles regarding your Dropbox data:

  • Access: We only access the files and folders necessary to provide the VDAM service (e.g., visual asset indexing).
  • No Unauthorized Sharing: We do not share, sell, or transfer your Dropbox data to any third-party data brokers, advertising networks, or unauthorized external services.
  • AI and Machine Learning: We do not use your private Dropbox files to train generalized AI models.

3. Data Storage and Retention

We retain your data only for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services. If you disconnect your Dropbox account or delete your VDAM account, we will systematically delete the associated indexed data from our active servers.

4. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data during transfer and at rest. Authentication with third-party services like Dropbox is handled via secure OAuth tokens, ensuring we never see or store your actual passwords.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us at: support@vdam.io
